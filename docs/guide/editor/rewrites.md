# Smart Rewriting

LoneWriter offers advanced tools to transform and polish your draft without leaving the writing flow. These functions are designed to act like a professional editor suggesting structural, stylistic, or tonal improvements.

---

## How to Use Rewriting
To activate the editing tools:
1. Select a text fragment in the editor.
2. Click the **Rewrite** icon (or use the configured keyboard shortcut).
3. Select the type of transformation you wish to apply.

---

## Available Rewriting Modes

### Style and Tone
- **Expand**: Adds descriptive details and sensory depth to the selected fragment.
- **Shorten**: Removes redundancies and improves conciseness without losing the essence of the message.
- **Change Tone**: Adjusts the fragment to be more dramatic, humorous, dark, or poetic.

### Correction and Structure
- **Polish Writing**: Corrects grammar, punctuation, and improves the rhythmic flow of the prose.
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Transforms explanatory passages into active and sensory descriptions.

---

## Change Comparison
When applying a rewrite, LoneWriter presents a comparative view:
- The original text is kept for your reference.
- The AI proposal is shown adjacently.
- You can choose **Accept**, which will replace the original text, or **Discard** to keep your initial version.

---

## Custom Configuration
It is possible to define custom instructions for rewrites in the app Settings. For example:
*"I want all my rewrites to avoid adverbs ending in -ly"* or *"Use technical vocabulary typical of hard science fiction."*

> [!TIP]
> For best results in stylistic rewrites, it is recommended to use models like `claude-3-5-sonnet`, whose literary capacity is superior.
