# Exporting Your Work

Author sovereignty implies the ability to move your work to any other platform without restrictions. LoneWriter facilitates the export of your texts and lore data in standard and open formats.

---

## Available Export Formats

### Novel Draft
You can export the full text or individual scenes in the following formats:
- **Markdown (.md)**: The standard for modern technical and creative writing. It preserves structure and is compatible with almost any editor.
- **Plain Text (.txt)**: Universal compatibility without formatting.
- **JSON**: For technical exports that include scene metadata.

### Compendium and Lore Data
Your world notes and relationships are not locked in the application:
- **JSON**: Full export of all entities, relationships, and tags. Ideal for manual backups or for developers wishing to process the data externally.
- **CSV**: Spreadsheet-compatible format to analyze the density of characters or locations.

---

## How to Perform an Export
1. Access the Project menu or **Settings** > **Export**.
2. Select the elements you want to include (Draft, Compendium, Timeline).
3. Choose the target format.
4. Click **Generate File**. The file will be downloaded directly to your computer.

---

## Manual Backups
It is recommended to perform a full export in JSON format after important work sessions. This file contains the entire structure of your universe and can be imported back into LoneWriter at any time, even on a clean browser installation.

> [!TIP]
> If you plan to typeset your novel for publication, exporting in Markdown is the most efficient option for importing text into professional typesetting tools or advanced word processors.
